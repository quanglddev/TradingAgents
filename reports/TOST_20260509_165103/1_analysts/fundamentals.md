## 2. Profitability and growth
- FY2025 revenue USD 6.153b, up 24.1% year over year from USD 4.960b; fourth quarter FY2025 (Q4 FY2025) revenue USD 1.633b, up 22.0% from Q4 FY2024 USD 1.338b. Growth strong, but decelerating from FY2024 +28.3% and FY2023 +41.5%.
- Gross margin expanded 18.7% FY2022 -> 21.6% FY2023 -> 24.0% FY2024 -> 25.9% FY2025. Q4 FY2025 gross margin 25.9% vs 24.9% Q4 FY2024. Gross profit rose 33.9% year over year in FY2025, faster than revenue.
- Operating leverage visible: operating margin -14.1% FY2022 -> -7.4% FY2023 -> 1.3% FY2024 -> 4.9% FY2025; Q4 FY2025 5.5% vs 2.4% Q4 FY2024. Selling, general, and administrative expense (SG&A) fell to 14.9% of revenue from 15.7%; research and development (R&D) fell to 6.1% from 7.1%.
- Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) margin improved to 6.0% FY2025 from 2.2% FY2024; net margin improved to 5.6% from 0.4%. Q4 FY2025 net margin 6.2% vs 2.4% Q4 FY2024.
- DuPont says improvement mostly margin-led: FY2025 net margin 5.6% × asset turnover 2.22× × leverage 1.51× ≈ 18.6% return on equity (ROE) on average-balance math; FY2025 net income / FY2024 equity = 22.1%. Turnover and leverage both softer versus FY2024, so margin did heavy lifting. Approx return on invested capital (ROIC), using earnings before interest and taxes (EBIT) after stated 1.2% tax on average invested capital, ~16.4% FY2025 vs ~4.0% FY2024.

## 6. Bull case / Bear case checklists
- Bull:
  - Revenue growth stays above 20% year over year; recent marks FY2025 +24.1%, Q4 FY2025 +22.0%.
  - Gross margin holds at or above 25.0%; FY2025 quarterly range sat 25.3%-26.5%.
  - Operating margin holds at or above 5.0%; Q2-Q4 FY2025 ran 5.1%-5.5%.
  - CFO / net income stays above 1.3× and free cash flow margin stays above 8.0%; FY2025 sat 1.93× and 9.9%.
  - Net cash stays above USD 1.5b; debt stays trivial versus cash.
  - Share growth slows to 2%-3% or lower, with buybacks at least matching issuance.
- Bear:
  - Revenue growth drops below 15% year over year.
  - Gross margin falls below 24.5% or operating margin below 4.0%; margin story breaks.
  - CFO / net income falls below 1.0× or free cash flow margin below 6.0%; accrual or working-capital risk rises.
  - SBC stays above 4.0% of revenue and ordinary shares grow above 3.0% year over year despite buybacks.
  - Accounts receivable growth exceeds revenue growth by more than 5 percentage points, or provisions/write-offs rise above USD 30m in quarter.
  - Net cash falls below USD 1.5b without clear return in growth or margins.
